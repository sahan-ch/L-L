About Lustre & Lore

At Lustre & Lore, we believe jewellery is more than an accessory. It is a story, a memory, and a piece of your spirit crafted into form. We create handmade, customized jewellery using ethically sourced gold, silver, and carefully selected natural gemstones. Every piece begins with a conversation to understand your feelings, your energy, and the story you want to carry.

From timeless elegance to contemporary artistry, our creations blend craftsmanship with meaning, allowing you to wear something truly personal. At Lustre & Lore, we do not just craft jewellery; we craft pieces that let your soul shine.

Our story — Lustre & Lore

Lustre & Lore was founded on the belief that jewellery becomes a companion that touches the skin, the mind, and the soul. When a jewel stays close to you, it can influence your confidence, your focus, and even the direction of your life. It becomes a silent friend that guides and reminds you of the person you wish to be.

Through research into design and natural gemstones, we explored how different stones and forms resonate with people. We combine practical knowledge and refined intuition to create pieces that harmonize with individual energy. Every soul is unique, and every piece we make is intended to reflect that individuality.

Our vision

To transform personal stories into heirloom jewellery by using timeless design, careful craft, and ethical materials.
