Privacy Policy

At Lustre & Lore, we value your trust. Your personal information is collected only to design your customized jewellery and to provide a safe and smooth service. We never misuse, sell, or share your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Your name, contact number, email address
  • Delivery address
  • Preferences shared during design discussions
  • Answers to our personality/energy questionnaire
  • Images or references you share for custom designs
  • Payment-related confirmations (no card details stored)

2. Why We Collect This Information

  • Create fully personalized jewellery
  • Select suitable metals and gemstones
  • Contact you regarding your order
  • Process advance payments and delivery
  • Provide a secure and trusted service
  • Improve your overall customer experience

We never collect unnecessary details.

3. How We Protect Your Information

We use secure systems to store your data. Only authorized team members can access it. We do not store sensitive financial information such as card numbers. All personal details are handled with strict confidentiality.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data with any third party. Information may be shared only when necessary, such as:

  • Delivery partners (only name, contact, address)
  • Payment verification (basic transaction details)

No other third parties can access your personal information.

5. Customer Rights

  • Ask what information we have about you
  • Request corrections
  • Request deletion after your order is completed
  • Withdraw consent for future communication

6. Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as needed for:

  • Completing your order
  • Legal and accounting requirements

After this, your data will be safely deleted.

7. Consent

By communicating with us or placing an order, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Refund Policy

Refunds

  • All jewellery pieces are custom-made based on the customer’s requirements.
  • Once a design is confirmed and work has begun, refunds or cancellations are not accepted.
  • This includes custom designs, personalized orders, or any item made uniquely for the customer.

After-Sales Service

  • Free or paid repairs depending on the nature of the damage
  • Stone replacement or stone changes (charges may apply)
  • Jewellery cleaning & polishing (as per our service policy)
  • Adjustments such as resizing (if possible for the item)

Manufacturing Defects

  • If there is a verified manufacturing defect, we will repair or remake the item at no additional cost.
  • Defects caused by misuse, accidents, chemical exposure, or normal wear & tear are not covered.
