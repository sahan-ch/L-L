Payment & Delivery

At L&L (Lustre & Lore), each jewellery piece is specially designed and handcrafted just for you. To begin the creation process, we require an advance payment of 25%–50% of the total value. The exact amount depends on the chosen metal and type of gemstone.

Once your design is confirmed and the advance is received, we will issue an official receipt for your trust and security. Your finished jewellery will be delivered to you through Cash on Delivery (COD), where you can pay the remaining balance at the time of delivery.

We ensure a smooth, transparent, and safe process from the first sketch to the moment you wear your personalised masterpiece.
